FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Calcium Nitrate used for?
Calcium Nitrate is a vital water-soluble compound providing fast-acting Calcium and Nitrogen. Used in agriculture (drip, foliar, hydroponics), it prevents fruit rot and blossom-end rot. Industrially, it's used in concrete, wastewater treatment, and latex processing.
Why is powder better than granules?
Powder dissolves instantly with zero residue. This prevents clogged drip lines, ensures 100% nutrient availability, and allows precise concentration control. Granules often contain insoluble coatings that remain in solution tanks.
