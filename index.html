Frequently Asked Questions

What is Calcium Nitrate used for?
Calcium Nitrate is a vital water-soluble fertilizer that provides fast-acting Calcium and Nitrogen. It improves cell wall strength in plants, prevents fruit rot, and is used industrially in concrete, wastewater treatment, and latex production.
Is powdered Calcium Nitrate better than granules?
Yes. While chemical composition is similar, powdered forms dissolve instantly without leaving residue. This prevents clogged drip lines and ensures 100% nutrient availability, unlike granules which often contain insoluble coatings.
