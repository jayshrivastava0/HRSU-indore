Applications

One molecule. Five industries.

01 - Agriculture & Horticulture

Eliminating deficiency at the root.

Prevents blossom-end rot, tip burn, and calcium deficiencies in tomatoes, peppers, and leafy greens. Compatible with all drip systems. Foliar-grade clarity - no nozzle clogging.

Drip Irrigation Foliar Spray Fertigation
02 - Hydroponics & Vertical Farms

The backbone of Tank A formulations.

Dissolves at room temperature - ideal for closed-loop NFT, DWC, and aeroponic systems demanding EC precision. Compatible with all A/B nutrient systems.

NFT Systems DWC Vertical Farms
03 - Construction & Concrete

Cold-weather pours. Zero corrosion risk.

Chloride-free accelerating admixture per ASTM C494. Reduces initial set time by up to 40%, enabling pours down to –15°C without reinforcement corrosion.

ASTM C494 Cold-Weather Accelerator
04 - Wastewater Treatment

Suppressing H₂S. Protecting infrastructure.

Controls hydrogen sulfide odour in municipal sewers and industrial effluent. Maintains denitrification balance in activated sludge processes.

H₂S Suppression Municipal ETP Denitrification
05 - Industrial Processing

Latex. Metal. Heat. Precise every time.

Latex coagulation, surface treatment in metal industries, and heat-transfer fluid formulations. Consistent industrial-grade batches with COA on every lot.

Latex Coagulation Metal Treatment COA Certified
